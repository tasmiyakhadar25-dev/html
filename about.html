AI-powered lung disease detection uses deep learning to analyze chest X-rays and identify abnormalities for early and accurate diagnosis. PREDICTION AND CLASSIFICATION OF FETAL HEALTH BY USING AI TECHNOLOGY

This project uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning algorithms on Cardiotocography (CTG) data to predict and classify fetal health as Normal, Suspect, or Pathological. It improves diagnostic accuracy, minimizes human error, and aids doctors in early detection of pregnancy- related complications.
